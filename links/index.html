Exploring the World of Literature

Classic novels have shaped the way we understand storytelling and human emotion. Works such as Pride and Prejudice explore societal expectations and love, while 1984 delves into control and surveillance. To Kill a Mockingbird highlights racial injustice and moral growth, Moby-Dick examines obsession and fate, and The Great Gatsby portrays the pursuit of the American Dream.
