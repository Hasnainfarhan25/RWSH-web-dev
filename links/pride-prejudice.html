Pride and Prejudice

Pride and Prejudice by Jane Austen is a timeless romantic novel that explores the themes of love, class, and social expectations in 19th-century England. It follows the spirited Elizabeth Bennet and the reserved Mr. Darcy as they navigate pride, prejudice, and personal growth.

  • Published: 1813
  • Genre: Romance, Satire
  • Main Characters: Elizabeth Bennet, Mr. Darcy, Jane Bennet
